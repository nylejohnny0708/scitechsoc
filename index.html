Current Climate Impacts

  • Extreme Weather: Increased frequency of hurricanes, droughts, floods, and wildfires
  • Sea Level Rise: Currently rising at 3.4mm per year, threatening coastal communities
  • Ocean Acidification: Oceans absorbing 25% of CO₂, harming marine ecosystems
  • Biodiversity Loss: 1 million species at risk of extinction due to habitat destruction
  • Heatwaves: Record-breaking temperatures affecting health and agriculture

Projected Consequences

  • Temperature Rise: Up to 4.4°C by 2100 without significant action
  • Sea Level Rise: Could reach 2 meters by 2100, displacing millions
  • Ecosystem Collapse: Coral reefs could disappear by 2050
  • Climate Migration: Over 1 billion people could be displaced by 2050
  • Economic Impact: $54 trillion in damages by 2100 if no action taken

Pathways to Solutions
